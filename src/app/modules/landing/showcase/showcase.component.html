CUSTOM EVENT EDITOR

No matter the size, Venuoso makes planning events easy using the custom Event Editor. Clients simply drag-and-drop to build the ideal event layout using your own inventory and then submit their creation directly to you, the venue for approval. Envisioning your clients perfect event has never been easier. After a client submits their layout, you are able to approve all the details and even accept payments instantly.

SEAMLESS COMMUNICATION

Are your customers plans getting too big for paper? Venuoso's built-in chat system allows customers to contact you directly to request special accommodations or discuss other requests. Contacting your clients has never been easier. Venue owners can instantly contact clients to discuss their approval status, important updates, billing, and much more.
